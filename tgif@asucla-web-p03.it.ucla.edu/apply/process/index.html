Funding Process

Interested in applying for a TGIF grant? Follow the steps below. There is also an infographic you can follow.

Am I eligible to apply?

  • I am a UCLA student, staff member, or faculty member (can be applying on behalf of an organization).

Does my project meet the requirements to apply to TGIF?

  • The project promotes environmental sustainability on UCLA's campus in accordance with our mission.
    • UCLA Single-Use Plastic Policy Implementation Guide. Check out page 23 for information on promotional products.
    • Green Events Certification Guide. Check out page 11 for information on commemorative products.
  • The project has a clearly-defined, measurable outcome.
  • The project has a mechanism for evaluation and follow-up after funding has been disbursed.
  • Learn more about TGIF guidelines.

The application process and expected timeline.

  • When creating your budget, reference this sample budget.
  • TGIF Mini Fund (requesting $1,000 or less)
    • Fill out an application before the deadline.
    • Within two weeks of the funding deadline, you will receive your allocation via email.
    • Funding closes Friday of Week 8 each quarter. If you miss a deadline, your application will be considered in the next funding round. No funding is awarded during the summer.
  • TGIF Main Fund (requesting $10,000 or less)
    • Fill out an application before the deadline.
    • Sign up for a hearing (the link will be sent a week following the deadline).
    • Attend the hearing.
    • Within two weeks of the hearing, you will receive your allocation via email.
    • Funding closes Friday of Week 2 each quarter. If you miss a deadline, your application will be considered in the next funding round. No funding is awarded during the summer.
  • Capital/Infrastructure Projects (requesting more than $10,000)
    • Fill out an application (accepted on a rolling basis).
    • Within two weeks of the funding deadline, you will receive your allocation via email.
    • If funded, you will schedule quarterly meetings with the Grant Coordinator.
    • Funding closes Friday of Week 8 Spring Quarter. If you miss this deadline, you must wait until the next academic year. No funding is awarded during the summer.
