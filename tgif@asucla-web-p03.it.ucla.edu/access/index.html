Access your Funding

You received your allocation email. Now what?

Grant agreement

  • Read through the Grant Agreement thoroughly and note your account number.
  • Return the signed form to tgif@asucla.ucla.edu.

Accessing funds through the Requisition Form

  • Fill out a requisition (Req) form.
    • Need help? See the step-by-step tutorial.
  • Submit it to the TGIF Grant Coordinator via email.
    • Make sure to attach your Req and supporting documentation.
    • If there is a problem with your form, we will contact you directly.
  • Check the Student Government Accounting (SGA) Check Register frequently to see if your funding is available.

Using your funds

  • Once your name appears on the Check Register, your funds are available!
  • Wait for the check to be sent to you, since the SGA office is currently closed due to COVID-19.
